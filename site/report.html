    9 * #### Notes for Through Constraints <span id="notes"></span>
    10 * Through constraints are used for many to many relationships. Actually there isn't such a thing called
    11 * **many to many relationship** or **through constraint** in the database engine. They are concepts to describe
    12 * records which may be related more than one record on both sides. For example an invoice may contain more than product and
    13 * a product may related to more than one invoice. Those relationships are solved a so called many to many **join table**.
    14 *
